Recipe For Avocado Egg Toast

Items Needed:

  • Two pieces of whole grain bread
  • One avocado
  • Two eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Hot honey
  1. First, toast 2 pieces of bread until golden
  2. Second, begin frying the eggs in olive oil until yolk is runny
  3. Third, mash the avocado and mix it with salt, pepper, and hot honey
  4. Lastly, assemble the toast by adding the avocado to the bread and topping it with the eggs
  5. Drizzle an additional amount of hot honey on top

Congrats! Now you have delicious avocado egg toast!
