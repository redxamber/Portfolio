Recipe For Chocolate Chip Cookies

Items Needed:

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • Two eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp of baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips
  1. Mix together the dry ingredients in a bowl - leaving out flour and chocolate chips
  2. Add wet ingredients into bowl and mix well
  3. Add flour into bowl in small increments until well combined
  4. Stir in chocolate chips
  5. Roll into balls and space out 3 inches on cookie sheet
  6. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes until golden brown

Congrats! Now you have delicious chocolate chip cookies!
